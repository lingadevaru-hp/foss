Free Open Source Soft




FOSS Coin is a community-created token built to support and promote Free and Open Source Software (FOSS).

It can be used to:
- Donate to FOSS projects
- Reward developers and contributors
- Raise awareness about digital privacy, Linux, and open technologies

This is not an official project by GNU, Linux.org, or the Free Software Foundation. $FOSS is an independent grassroots effort by passionate individuals who believe in the freedom to build, share, and innovate without restrictions.

By holding or using FOSS Coin, you become part of a decentralized movement to fund the tools that power the internet — freely and ethically.
